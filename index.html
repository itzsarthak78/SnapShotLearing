Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a course?
Browse courses, click on any course, and tap "Buy Now". Complete payment via Razorpay to get instant access.
How do I become a seller?
Go to Profile → Sell Your Course. Create a course and submit for admin approval. Once approved, your course goes live.
Are payments refundable?
Payments are non-refundable unless stated in the course description or a technical issue prevents access.
How long does course approval take?
Admin reviews within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your course is approved or if it needs changes.
